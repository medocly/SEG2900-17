The story behind Medocly

There isn't really good self diagnosis apps out there... To be honest, a lot of the solutions offered right now just straight up don’t work. But what happens when you really need to see a doctor, but you don’t have access to one? Or if you only want to do a quick little checkup but don’t want to go through the hassle of calling a doctor, getting an appointment and then waiting, waiting and waiting again? Or what if you need to have some medication, but you’re simply too tired or sick to go buy it yourself? We all have a ton of reasons why we would prefer to have a more convenient wait to deal with this. Those are the reasons why we decided to create Medocly.
Here at Medocly, we believe that anybody should have access to a good and reliable diagnosis. We also believe that anybody should be able to get the right medication fast

Description of Features

Medication Delivery


Order medications which have been prescribed
and have them delivered to you!

Online Payment



Users can pay with a simple tap!

Map


Locate medical centres near you for your unique needs!

Connection with Diagnostic Device




Tired of waiting for an appointment or don't have time? Users are able to pay for a subscription to use a feature that connects to a medical device which diagnoses your symptoms and gives immediate feedback.

Privacy


Privacy is our number one priority. Your medical data is not shared with anyone. We value confidentiality and will have a lock system in place so your deliveries remain private.

Analytical Reports


Summaries of overall factors like blood pressure are sugar levels are provided to the user.
